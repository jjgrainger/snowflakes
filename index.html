About Snowflakes

A present from me to you! Type your name into the field at the top and turn your name into a snowflake. Each snowflake is unique to whatever you type.

You can shuffle through random snowflakes with the random button. If you see a snowflake you like, you can save an image of the snowflake with the download button and share it with your friends and family!
